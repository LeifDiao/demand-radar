Plus加分The pain is real痛点真实
"Notion is too complex" recurs across 4 independent sources (HN, App Store, Medium). Users even ask Notion to ship an official "simple mode."「Notion 太复杂」在 HN、App Store、Medium 等 4 个独立来源反复出现，用户甚至主动求官方出「简单模式」。
Plus加分The category makes money品类能赚钱
Slite at $5.5M ARR; Coda acquired by Grammarly on $41M revenue. "Simple collaborative tool" is a proven, paying model.Slite 年收入 $5.5M、Coda 以 $41M 营收被 Grammarly 收购。「简单协作工具」是被验证过、有人付费的模式。
Minus减分Buyer mismatch · fatal买家错配 · 致命
The people who pick the tool and pay are admins / founders. The non-technical members you target neither decide nor hold budget — so nobody who can buy is in the room.选工具、付钱的是团队 admin / 创始人。你瞄准的非技术成员既不掌握选型权，也不掌握预算——做出来，能买单的人根本不在场。
Minus减分Already a red ocean已是红海
100+ "simpler Notion" alternatives already ship; Coda, Slite, Nuclino own "team + simple." "Simpler" on its own is not a wedge.市面已有 100+ 个主打「更简单」的 Notion 替代品，Coda、Slite、Nuclino 已占住「团队 + 简单」格子。光靠「更简单」切不进去。
Watch注意A vitamin, not a painkiller是维生素，不是止痛药
The pain is annoying-yet-bearable. A 20-person team needs 2–4 weeks to migrate; $1,800/yr, non-refundable. Switching cost dwarfs the pain, so it won't trigger a paid switch.痛点是「烦但能忍」。20 人团队迁移要 2–4 周、年费 $1800 不退——切换成本远高于痛苦，不足以让人付费换。
Watch注意Churn goes to free, not to paid流失奔的是免费
In a real migration, a 15-person team moved back to free Apple Notes; adoption fell from 14/15 to 3/15. Churned users flee to free, not to a new paid product.真实迁移里，15 人团队整体退回免费的 Apple Notes，采用率从 14/15 跌到 3/15。流失的人奔的是免费，不是新付费产品。
